ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Shrewsbury Massachusetts, just outside of Worcester. From a young age, my mother stayed home and helped me and my brother learn how to read and write to prepare us for school. Since elemantary school I worked hard and found enjoyment in math and science. Throughout middle and high school, I had slowly figured out what precisly I enjoyed about math and science. I love problem solving and those moments when I'm stuck but then finally have figure it out and see the result. The major that is best for this enjoyment is definitely computer science. There is no better feeling than spending hours working out bugs on a project and then seeing it all come together to work.

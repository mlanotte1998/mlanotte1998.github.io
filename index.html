ABOUT ME

I'm a second year student at Northeastern University going for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with minors in Math and Electrical Engineering.

Born and raised in Shrewsbury Massachusetts, just outside of Worcester. From a young age, my mother stayed home and helped my brother and I learn how to read and write to prepare us for school. Since elementary school, I worked hard and found enjoyment in math and science. Throughout middle and high school, I slowly figured out what precisely I enjoyed about math and science. I love problem solving and those moments when I'm stuck but then finally figure it out and see the result. The major that constantly has these type of moments is definitely computer science. There is no better feeling than spending hours working out bugs on a project and then seeing it all come together to work.

Sports

I played football and ran track in highschool. I workout regularly and play basketball with friends in my free time. I have been snowboarding since I was eight and worked as a snowboard instructor for four years. My favorite sport to watch is college basketball.

Tech

I have been extremely interested in the latest technology for my entire life. I almost always knew I wanted to be some type of engineer but only when I was seventeen did I start my path towards software/computer science and so far I love everything about it.

Video Games

My favorite industry to follow is the gaming industry. Learning about studios and the people behind games is important to me. If you need to know when a game is coming out or who made it, I'm the guy to talk to. Also, you can likely tell that I'm a huge fallout fan.
